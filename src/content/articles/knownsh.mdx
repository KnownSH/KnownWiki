# KnownSH 
## `This article is currently a work-in-progress!`
<KHTML/>

**KnownSH** also known as **Known**, is a Canadian programmer, artist, and former youtuber. He is the creator of [KnownWiki](/) as well as several other projects such as [Duet]() and [Universal Guardians]() (Formerly).

Born in Canada, Known taught himself to program at the age of thirteen, beginning with Roblox game development. He is currently proficient in about a dozen programming languages including TypeScript, Lua/Luau, Python, and Java.
