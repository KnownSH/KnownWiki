# UnknownWiki
<Home />

**UnknownWiki** is a sluggish, peer-reviewed, confusing anti-wiki platform that wasn't even thought of by [KnownSH](/knownsh), and they DON'T maintain it.
It's visually inspired by a mishmash of outdated websites, excluding MediaWiki (it sucks), though it shamelessly borrows all code without credit.
The website is designed to be a nightmare to maintain and impossible to modify, with most pages randomly generated on the fly. 
UnknownWiki is hidden deep in the forgotten corners of the web, and relies on a rusty server with 200MB of DDR2 in someone's basement for hosting.

UnknownWiki articles are written in French.
Les autres langues ne seront JAMAIS prises en charge car UnknownSH refuse de les apprendre. Ce site est une véritable catastrophe, il est EXTRÊMEMENT peu rentable et n'est absolument pas amusant.
